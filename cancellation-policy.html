Cancellation Policy

Please read the appointment and cancellation guidance before booking

Appointment Guidance

Notice period

Please aim to give at least 24 to 48 hours’ notice if you need to cancel or rearrange an appointment.

Late cancellation or no-show

Where appointments are missed without notice or cancelled very late, the full session fee may be charged.

Exceptions

Allowance may be made for emergencies, sudden illness, or rare unexpected circumstances where appropriate.

Late arrival

If a client is more than 15 minutes late without prior notice, the session may be treated as missed.

Written agreement

Cancellation expectations are normally included within the intake and service agreement so expectations are clear from the outset.
