Integrative Counselling

Janine blends different therapeutic approaches to suit your unique situation. This means your therapy is shaped around your needs, rather than forcing you into one fixed model.

  • ✓ Flexible, personalised care
  • ✓ Draws on CBT, person-centred, trauma-informed work
  • ✓ Ideal for complex or changing needs
  • ✓ Online video sessions only

Solution-Focused Therapy

This approach helps you identify what is working well and build practical steps toward the future you want. It is especially useful when you want clear progress and structure in fewer sessions.

  • ✓ Goal-focused conversations
  • ✓ Strength-based support
  • ✓ Practical coping strategies
  • ✓ Useful for anxiety, depression and life stress
Schedule a Session

Person-Centred Therapy

A warm, non-judgemental space where you are listened to with empathy and respect. This therapy is designed to help you feel understood and supported as you explore your thoughts and feelings.

  • ✓ Empathy-first approach
  • ✓ Safe, accepting environment
  • ✓ Encourages self-awareness and growth
  • ✓ Supports identity and self-acceptance
Schedule a Session

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

CBT helps you understand how your thoughts, feelings and behaviours are connected. Janine uses this practical approach to support coping strategies, grounding techniques and changes in day-to-day thinking.

  • ✓ Thought pattern awareness
  • ✓ Behavioural tools and exercises
  • ✓ Anxiety and depression support
  • ✓ Promotes realistic, helpful thinking

Trauma-Informed Support

Experience gentle, respectful care that recognises the impact of trauma on your mind and body. Janine creates a safe pace for healing while helping you feel grounded and understood.

  • ✓ Safety and choice focused
  • ✓ Sensitive trauma awareness
  • ✓ Grounding and coping tools
  • ✓ Suitable for long-term recovery
