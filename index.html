My Journey

Hi, I’m Janine — a counsellor who listens, understands and supports you with care.

I have always been the person my family and friends turned to when they needed someone who would really listen. That natural instinct to care led me into work supporting children with SEND, and later into training as an integrative counsellor and psychotherapist.

Today I offer online sessions designed to feel warm, calm and completely non-judgemental. I’m an MBACP member with Level 5 training and I’m working towards my BA. I support people with anxiety, depression, long COVID, stroke recovery, identity questions, self-acceptance and grounding strategies.
