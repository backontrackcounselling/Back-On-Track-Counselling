About Back on Track

Your trusted partner in mental health and personal growth

Our Mission

At Back on Track Counselling, we believe that everyone deserves access to professional, compassionate mental health support. Our mission is to help individuals, couples, and families navigate their challenges, overcome obstacles, and create meaningful, fulfilling lives.

Our Vision

We envision a world where mental health is destigmatized, mental health support is accessible to all, and people feel empowered to take control of their wellbeing. We're committed to providing evidence-based, personalized counselling that meets you where you are.

Our Values

Compassion

We approach every client with empathy, respect, and genuine care for their wellbeing.

Confidentiality

Your privacy is sacred. We maintain the highest standards of professional confidentiality.

Evidence-Based

We use proven therapeutic techniques and stay current with the latest research.

Client-Centered

Your goals and needs drive our approach to therapy and counselling.
