Privacy Policy

How personal information is handled with care, transparency, and respect

Your Privacy Matters

Who we are

Back on Track Counselling provides professional support and online appointment booking. General enquiries can be made by email or phone using the contact details shown on this website.

What information may be collected

This may include your name, email address, telephone number, appointment details, and any personal information you choose to provide when making contact.

Why information is used

Information is used to reply to enquiries, arrange appointments, deliver services, keep appropriate records, and maintain the safe functioning of the website.

Lawful basis

Information is processed only where there is a proper legal basis under UK data protection law, including responding to your request, providing services, or meeting professional and legal obligations.

Who information may be shared with

Where necessary, information may be shared with trusted service providers such as secure hosting, email, or booking systems, or where there is a safeguarding or legal obligation to do so.

Your rights

You may request access to your personal information, ask for corrections, and raise concerns about how information is used. You also have the right to contact the ICO if needed.
