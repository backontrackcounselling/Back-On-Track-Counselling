Frequently Asked Questions

How much does counselling cost?

Individual sessions are £60 for 50 minutes. We offer package discounts: 5-session packages for £280 or 10-session packages for £550. Many insurance plans cover our services.

Is online counselling as effective as in-person?

Research shows online counselling is equally effective as in-person sessions. The key is finding a format that makes you comfortable and able to be open.

What is the cancellation policy?

We require 48 hours notice for cancellations. Cancellations with less notice may be charged. We understand life happens and will work with you on exceptions.

How long does therapy typically last?

This varies based on individual needs. Some clients benefit from short-term counselling (6-12 sessions) while others continue longer-term. We'll discuss what's right for you.

Is everything I say confidential?

Yes, with very few legal exceptions (if you're at risk of harming yourself or others). We follow strict confidentiality standards as required by law.

Can I request a specific counsellor?

Absolutely! You can request a specific counsellor during booking. If your preferred counsellor isn't available, we'll suggest someone who might be a great fit.
